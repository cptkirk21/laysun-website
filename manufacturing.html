Materials

What Makes LaySun Plants Different

Material selection is where realism begins. We use only high-grade PE and hybrid compounds developed specifically for commercial artificial plants.

🧪

High-Grade PE (Polyethylene)

Our signature material. PE captures the softness, translucency, and vein detail of real leaves. UV-stable and resistant to fading for 10+ years in interior conditions.

🌲

Natural Wood Trunks

Real dried wood trunks are treated, sealed, and shaped to create authentic tree forms. No plastic trunks — the texture and weight is genuine.

🔥

Fire-Retardant Treatment

All foliage undergoes a proprietary fire-retardant treatment process. Tested and certified to NFPA 701 (USA), EN 13501 (EU), and B1 (China) standards.

☀️

UV-Resistant Coating

Outdoor and high-light products receive UV-stabilized pigments and clear coat treatments, preventing color degradation even in direct sunlight.

🪨

Weighted Base Systems

Fiber-reinforced concrete or steel base plates ensure stability for freestanding trees and planters in high-traffic environments.

📦

Export-Grade Packaging

Custom foam-lined export cartons protect every order during international shipping. Full packing lists and customs documentation included.

How It's Made

Our Manufacturing Process

1

Design & Specification

Every project begins with detailed specs — species, dimensions, vessel type, and fire standard required. Our design team creates technical drawings before production begins.

2

PE Mold Production

Foliage is produced from proprietary molds using high-grade PE resin. Leaf shapes, vein structures, and color gradients are engineered into the mold for maximum realism.

3

Hand-Shaping & Assembly

Skilled craftspeople hand-shape each branch and leaf cluster. Branches are wrapped with natural fiber tape and arranged to mimic authentic plant growth patterns.

4

Fire Treatment

Each unit is immersed in or sprayed with our fire-retardant compound. Batch testing is performed against NFPA 701, EN 13501, or B1 standards as required by the destination market.

5

Quality Inspection

A 12-point quality checklist is completed for every unit — covering realism, structural integrity, fire certification, and packaging. Photos are shared with clients pre-shipment.

6

Export & Delivery

Orders are packed in custom export cartons with foam protection. We manage all export documentation, customs declarations, and arrange freight worldwide.

Certifications

Standards We Meet

Our fire-rated products are tested and certified to major international standards. Documentation is provided with every commercial order.

🇺🇸

NFPA 701

USA standard for flame resistance of textiles and films. Required for most US commercial properties.

🇪🇺

EN 13501

European fire classification standard for construction products. Class B & C ratings available.

🇨🇳

GB 8624 B1

Chinese national fire safety standard. B1 (flame-retardant) certification for all PE products.

🌍

Custom Testing

We work with accredited labs to meet additional regional standards on request. Contact us with your requirements.
