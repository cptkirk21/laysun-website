Top 5 Trends Dominating 2026

Based on our work with hotels, offices, and restaurants, here are the trends we're seeing most frequently:

1. Biophilic Design Integration

Companies are moving beyond simple plant placement to create full-scale biophilic environments. We're seeing demand for:

  • Full-ceiling hanging installations
  • Custom green walls with integrated lighting
  • Oversized statement palms and ficus
  • Multi-level plant arrangements

2. Custom Green Walls for Brand Differentiation

Restaurants and hotels are using custom green walls as signature elements that define their brand. These aren't just decoration ‚Äì they're strategic branding tools.

  • Brand-specific color schemes
  • Custom plant selections for themed environments
  • Integrated lighting for dramatic effects

Restaurant signature wall with custom artificial greenery

3. Fire Safety Compliance as Priority

With increased focus on commercial fire safety, we're seeing higher demand for:

  • NFPA 701 compliant materials
  • Ultra-violet (UV) resistant plants for outdoor use
  • Documented fire ratings and certifications

4. Outdoor Durability and Weather Resistance

For rooftop bars, poolside areas, and exterior spaces, durability is key. Trends include:

  • PE polyethylene materials for superior outdoor performance
  • Weather-resistant foliage that won't fade or deteriorate
  • Sturdy construction for high-wind areas

5. Sustainability and MaintenancT¥Free Solutions

Companies are seeking solutions that align with their sustainability goals while eliminating maintenance costs:

  • Artificial plants as part of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) reporting
  • WATER-FREE alternatives to live plants
  • Long-lasting solutions that don't require replacement
