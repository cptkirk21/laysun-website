Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: March 3, 2026

LaySun ("we", "us", or "our") is a manufacturer of fire-rated PE artificial plants headquartered at No. 25 Qiaofeng Road, Qishi Town, Dongguan, Guangdong, China. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you visit our website (laysun.co) or interact with us for business purposes.

By using our website or submitting an inquiry, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you complete a contact form, request a quote, or send us an email, we may collect:

  • Your name and job title
  • Company name and business address
  • Email address and phone number
  • Details of your project or product inquiry
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain data is collected automatically by our analytics tools, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type and operating system

1.3 Analytics — Apollo.io Website Tracker

Our website uses the Apollo.io website tracker (App ID: 699435a6d52bb100156127ec) to collect visitor data and help us understand who is visiting our site. Apollo.io may identify company-level visitor information (such as the organization a visitor belongs to) using IP intelligence and third-party data sources. This is used solely for B2B sales and marketing purposes. Apollo.io's privacy policy is available at apollo.io/privacy-policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes
  • Process and manage product orders
  • Communicate about your order status, shipping, and delivery
  • Improve our website and understand which content is most useful to visitors
  • Identify potential business customers and follow up on sales leads
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data for purposes unrelated to our business operations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate interests: To respond to business inquiries and conduct B2B marketing activities.
  • Contract performance: To fulfill orders and manage business relationships.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly consented, such as by submitting a contact form.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Personal data submitted through contact forms and order processes is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law — typically no longer than 5 years from the date of last contact.

5. Third-Party Processors

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and business, including:

  • Apollo.io — website visitor analytics and B2B lead intelligence
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (no personal data retained)
  • Email service providers — for processing inquiries and communications
  • Freight forwarders and logistics partners — for order delivery and customs documentation

All third-party processors are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. For full details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Rights

7.1 GDPR Rights (EEA Residents)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Request portability of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

7.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

8. International Data Transfers

As a China-based company, your data may be transferred to and processed in China or other countries where our processors operate. We take steps to ensure that international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including using standard contractual clauses where required.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed exclusively at business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

To exercise your data rights, ask a question about this policy, or submit a data request, please contact us:

  • Email: info@laysun.co
  • Address: No. 25 Qiaofeng Road, Qishi Town, Dongguan, Guangdong, China
  • WhatsApp: +1 213-829-8485

We will respond to all legitimate data requests within 30 days.
