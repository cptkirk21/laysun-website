Who We Are

The Commercial Standard for Artificial Greenery

LaySun manufactures premium PE artificial plants engineered specifically for commercial environments. Every product is crafted with fire-rated materials, realistic textures, and built to last decades — not years.

From custom 10-foot olive trees for luxury hotel lobbies to full green-wall installations for corporate offices, we handle every project factory-direct — eliminating middlemen and delivering exceptional value.

  • NFPA 701 & B1 fire-rated materials
  • Natural wood trunks and hand-shaped foliage
  • Custom sizing and species matching
  • Factory-direct pricing — 30–50% below traditional suppliers
