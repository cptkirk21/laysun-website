Our Story

From a small factory in China to a globally trusted supplier of commercial artificial plants — here's how LaySun became the standard.

Who We Are

Built Around One Belief: Artificial Should Look Real

LaySun was founded on the conviction that artificial plants for commercial spaces didn't have to look artificial. For too long, hotels and restaurants settled for plastic imitations that fooled no one. We set out to change that.

Today, LaySun plants are installed in five-star hotels, Michelin-starred restaurants, and Fortune 500 headquarters across 50+ countries. The most common compliment we receive? "Guests keep asking if they're real."

We operate as a factory-direct supplier — meaning clients get better quality at lower prices, with no middlemen inflating costs or diluting quality control.

Our Values

What Drives Everything We Do

🎯

Realism First

Every product decision — material, shape, color, texture — is made with one question: "Does this look real?" We won't ship anything that doesn't pass that test.

🔥

Safety Always

Commercial spaces carry real fire risk. We treat fire certification as a baseline requirement, not an optional add-on. Every LaySun product is testable and certifiable.

🏭

Factory Direct

We don't use distributors or agents. Clients talk directly with the people who make the product — which means faster responses, cleaner pricing, and no miscommunication.

🌍

Global Reach

We ship to 50+ countries and understand the logistics, customs requirements, and fire standards of major markets worldwide.

🤝

Long-Term Relationships

Most of our business comes from repeat clients and referrals. We invest in getting the first project right so clients want to come back for the next ten.

🌱

Conscious Production

Artificial plants, done right, reduce the environmental cost of frequent plant replacement. We're committed to durable products that replace the need for constant consumption.

Our History

How LaySun Grew

11

2011 — Founded

LaySun is established as a small artificial plant manufacturer with a focus on quality over quantity. First clients: local interior designers and hospitality contractors.

14

2014 — First Export Orders

International clients discover LaySun through trade shows. First export orders ship to Southeast Asia and the Middle East. The factory expands its production floor.

17

2017 — Fire Certification Program Launched

Responding to growing demand from US and EU hospitality clients, LaySun invests in a dedicated fire-treatment line and obtains NFPA 701 and EN 13501 certifications.

21

2021 — 100th Project Milestone

LaySun completes its 100th commercial project. Client base expands to include hotel groups, restaurant chains, and corporate real estate firms across 30+ countries.

25

2025 — 300+ Projects, 50+ Countries

LaySun today operates a 50,000 sq ft facility, produces 600+ plant varieties, and has completed more than 300 commercial projects worldwide. The factory-direct model remains at the core of everything.

Our Model

Why Factory-Direct Changes Everything

Traditional artificial plant supply chains add 2–3 layers of margin between the factory and the end client. Each layer adds cost and creates distance between who makes the product and who uses it.

LaySun removes those layers entirely. When you work with us, you're working directly with the engineers, craftspeople, and quality managers who build your products.
