Plant Systems

Six distinct artificial plant systems engineered for commercial environments — from statement trees to full green-wall ecosystems.

Our Approach

Purpose-Built Systems, Not Just Products

Every LaySun system is designed as a complete solution — from the base and vessel to the foliage and fire-rating certification. We don't sell single plants; we engineer environments.

All systems are available in custom sizes, species, and color variations. Our team works with your design specs to deliver exactly what the rendering shows.

  • All materials NFPA 701 and/or EN 13501 fire-rated
  • Custom sizing from desktop to 20-ft ceiling height
  • Full documentation for fire inspector sign-off
  • Installation guides and video support included
