Industry Solutions

Purpose-designed artificial plant solutions for every commercial sector — each optimised for the specific demands of that environment.

Why It Matters

The Right Plant System for Your Industry

A hotel lobby, a restaurant terrace, and a hospital reception all have different fire codes, aesthetic standards, and maintenance expectations. LaySun designs solutions for each.

🔥

Fire-Code Compliance

Every sector has different fire standards. We supply the right certification — NFPA 701, EN 13501, or B1 — for your region and venue type.

🎨

Aesthetic Match

We work from your design briefs, mood boards, or existing décor to select species, colors, and vessels that complete your space perfectly.

📋

Zero Ongoing Cost

Every LaySun system eliminates maintenance — no watering schedules, no seasonal replacements, no specialist gardeners. Set it and forget it.
