Fire Safety

Every Product. Full Certification.

All LaySun commercial products are NFPA 701 (USA), EN 13501 (Europe), and GB 8624 B1 (China) certified. Fire-retardant additives are blended into the PE compound at manufacture — not surface-sprayed — so certification holds at every annual inspection.

Test certificates are included with every commercial order, in the format required by your jurisdiction's fire inspector.
