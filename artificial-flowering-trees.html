Commercial Artificial Flowering Trees

12 flowering varieties across camellia, hydrangea, bougainvillea and cherry blossom — from 2.5 to 8 ft. Permanent colour, zero petal drop, NFPA 701 fire-rated for hotels, restaurants and events.

Why Commercial Artificial Flowering Trees Outperform the Real Thing

Real flowering trees in commercial environments create a maintenance nightmare that most operations teams cannot sustain. A real camellia in a hotel lobby drops petals continuously during bloom, requiring daily clean-up on polished floors — a slip hazard and a housekeeping burden. Real cherry blossom trees have a bloom window of two to three weeks per year, leaving eleven months of bare branches. Real hydrangeas wilt within days without precise humidity and watering, and real bougainvillea drops bracts at the slightest environmental change. Commercial artificial flowering trees solve every one of these problems: permanent colour at full bloom 365 days a year, zero petal or bract drop, no irrigation, no pollination, no pest risk, and full fire certification for any public hospitality space. The visual impact of a 6 ft artificial camellia tree in a hotel reception — cascading red or white blooms from floor to ceiling — is achievable today without a single maintenance call after installation.

Camellia Trees for Hotel Lobbies and Reception Areas

The artificial camellia tree is our most versatile commercial flowering tree. Available in red, white, pink, and yellow across sizes from 2.5 ft to 7 ft, camellia trees work as reception desk feature plants, corridor accent pieces, restaurant table-adjacent statement plants, and large lobby anchors. The red camellia is the most dramatic — a 6 or 7 ft specimen in a hotel lobby commands attention from the moment a guest enters. White camellia trees are the preferred choice for luxury bridal events, wedding venue dressing, and high-end spa reception areas where a clean, sophisticated aesthetic is required. The pink and yellow variants offer options for seasonal colour theming and branded interior colour schemes.

Cherry Blossom Trees for Events, Weddings, and Seasonal Displays

The commercial artificial cherry blossom tree is the most requested product for event hire companies, wedding venue operators, and exhibition display contractors. A pair of 5 or 6 ft pink cherry blossom trees flanking an event entrance creates an immediate and memorable visual statement with a fraction of the cost and complexity of sourcing real blooming specimens. White cherry blossom trees are the preferred choice for winter and spring wedding themes, while the red cherry blossom offers a striking option for Chinese New Year and Lunar New Year commercial displays. Because LaySun's PE blossom clusters are individually wired, each tree can be shaped and adjusted on-site by the installing team to achieve exactly the silhouette required.

Hydrangea Trees, Bougainvillea, and Fire Certification

Hydrangea trees — in pink, purple, and blue — are the preferred choice for restaurant table-area features and corporate function space dressing. Their lush, domed flower heads read as abundant and celebratory without the overwhelming scale of a full-height flowering tree. At 4 to 5 ft, a hydrangea tree fits perfectly beside dining tables, in corridors, and in private dining rooms. Bougainvillea trees bring a Mediterranean terrace aesthetic to commercial interiors: the real wood trunk bougainvillea at 6 to 8 ft is specified for restaurant terrace dressing, hospitality tent interiors, and large event spaces where a holiday-resort atmosphere is the design brief. All LaySun commercial artificial flowering trees carry NFPA 701, EN 13501, and GB 8624 B1 fire-retardant certification — critical for any flowering plant used in a licensed venue where fire authority inspection applies. PE blooms incorporate fire-retardant additives at the manufacturing stage. Colour selection guidance and custom pot finish options are available on request.
