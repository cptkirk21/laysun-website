Commercial Artificial Olive Trees

20 styles from 3 to 10 ft. Premium PE foliage, natural wood trunk options, and spherical 3D canopies. Every tree is fire-rated to NFPA 701 and EN 13501 — factory-direct, shipped worldwide.

Why Artificial Olive Trees Dominate Commercial Interiors

The commercial artificial olive tree has become the defining statement plant of the decade — and for good reason. Hotels, upscale restaurants, corporate headquarters, and retail flagships across North America, Europe, and the Middle East specify artificial olive trees above every other species. Their silvery-green foliage, gnarled trunks, and Mediterranean character communicate warmth and sophistication without the maintenance burden of a living plant. Unlike a real olive tree, a commercial-grade artificial olive tree never drops leaves onto polished floors, never requires irrigation systems, and never wilts under the stress of an HVAC draft or low-light interior.

PE Foliage vs PVC — Why Material Choice Matters for Olive Trees

Not all artificial olive tree foliage is equal. PVC leaves are flat, shiny, and obviously synthetic up close — acceptable for budget residential use but immediately visible as fake in a high-end commercial environment. LaySun's commercial artificial olive trees use PE (polyethylene) foliage moulded directly from botanical specimens. Each leaf captures the characteristic dusty silver-green underside and slight curl of a genuine olive leaf. When lit by warm hospitality lighting at 3–4 metres away, PE olive foliage is indistinguishable from the real thing. The PE compound also incorporates fire-retardant additives at the manufacturing stage — not surface-sprayed — so the fire certification remains valid for the life of the product.

Trunk Styles for Every Commercial Setting

Trunk selection is as important as foliage quality in a commercial artificial olive tree. LaySun offers four primary trunk constructions: the real natural wood trunk (harvested, treated, and affixed to the steel armature) for the most organic appearance; the old stump thick trunk style for a dramatic, aged Mediterranean look suited to luxury hotel lobbies; the straight black trunk for contemporary minimalist restaurants and corporate offices; and the white pot presentation that integrates with modern interior design schemes. For large commercial atriums requiring trees above 8 ft, the wide canopy and large tree head options provide the visual volume needed to fill double-height spaces. The 9 ft and 10 ft large tree variants are frequently specified for hotel check-in lobbies, airport lounges, and shopping centre anchor points.

Fire Rating, Certification, and Commercial Compliance

Every LaySun commercial artificial olive tree meets NFPA 701 (USA), EN 13501 (Europe), and GB 8624 B1 (China) fire-retardant standards. Test certificates are provided with every commercial order in the format required by your jurisdiction's fire inspector. This is particularly important for fake olive trees specified in commercial settings — most US municipalities, UK fire authorities, and European hospitality licensing bodies require documented fire certification for any artificial plants used in public-facing spaces. Fire-retardant additives are blended into the PE compound at manufacture, not surface-sprayed after the fact, ensuring the rating holds through annual inspections. Customisation options include custom pot finishes, bespoke trunk heights, specific canopy diameter requirements, and multi-tree configurations for corridors and planted zones. All custom specifications are accommodated at the factory with a typical lead time of 30–45 days.
